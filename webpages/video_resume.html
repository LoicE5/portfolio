Video Resume

Overview

A video resume is an original self-introduction performed in a short video (usually less than three minutes). The goal is to show the most of our capabilities in a dynamic way and in a short timestamp. The usage of visual effects, compositing, chroma key and such techniques is relevant for a video resume.

My video resume project is shot on green screen, which allow a better illustration of my sayings. Moreover, this brings another dimension to the resume, making it more dynamic.
